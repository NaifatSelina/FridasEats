Our Passion

Fridas' Eats is a 5 star restaurant located in Mayfair, London, serving the upmost authentic Zanzibari food, our aim is to be unique flavour, dishes and a unique dining experience to your fingertips, right in the heart of London.

Our menu includes a wide range of Zanzibari dishes prepared freshly everyday, from our lunch menu to our al carte and dessert menu, there is something for everyone. Fresh seafood and Mishkaki bought to the table sizzling are just two of the delicacies on our menu.

Our restaurant aims to provide a relaxing atmosphere and a fine dining experience to you. When stepping in to Fridas' Eats you'll be transported to a Zanzibar with the choice of music, various pieces of Zanzibari art sourced from Stone Town covering the walls and the low lit ambience and tropic decor to ensure a highly relaxing experience.

In fast-paced London, take a break and indulge in an island inspired dining experience.
